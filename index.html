About Pérez Zeledón

Pérez Zeledón is the 19th canton of the province of San José in Costa Rica, located in the Brunca region.

The canton of Pérez Zeledón was established October 9th, 1931

The canton of Pérez Zeledón's economic development has been characterized mostly by agriculture, livestock, trade, industry and tourism.

Commercial activities have emerged with boom in recent decades, largely due to the establishment of credit unions, banks, local companies, media, and various other companies of the central valley (San José) setting operations in the canton.
